You will be playing this game with another person pretending to be your colleague. The game lasts for a maximum of {{ Constants.num_rounds }} rounds.

Every morning you arrive at work between 8:30 am and 9:10 am. Your colleague will arrive either 5 minutes before, at the same time as you, or 5 minutes after. Example: You arrive at 8:47 am. Your colleague can arrive at 8:42 am, 8:47 am, or 8:52 am.

If you arrive before 9:00 am, you have time to go to the canteen for a cup of coffee. If you arrive at or after 9:00 am, you have to go straight to your office. You should only go to the canteen if your colleague also does. As you will only find out where your colleague went after you've made your own choice, you will have to try guess what your colleague is going to do.

At the beginning of each round you will see your own arrival time and you will then have to decide whether to go to the canteen or to your office.

Scoring:

  • Each round your bonus are calculated as follows:
    • If both of you go to the canteen before 9:00 am, you get {{ Constants.win_canteen|c }}.
    • If both of you go to your offices, you get {{ Constants.win_office|c }}, no matter what time it is.
  • You will lose the game and all of your bonuses if:
    • One of you goes to the canteen while the other goes to their office.
    • If one (or both) of you goes to the canteen at 9:00 am or later.
